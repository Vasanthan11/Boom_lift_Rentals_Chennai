Boom Lift Types – Chennai Fleet

Choose the right boom lift rental for your Chennai site

Articulated (Knuckle) Boom Lifts

For “up-and-over” access with obstacles

Ideal where you need to reach over parapets, ducts or signage frames. Articulated booms use jointed sections to move around obstacles and access awkward spots on Chennai sites.

Common uses: façade repairs, exterior cleaning, electrical and piping work around structures.

Telescopic (Straight) Boom Lifts

For maximum horizontal reach

Telescopic boom lifts extend in a straight line and are suited to open job sites that need high vertical and long horizontal reach – such as flyovers, tall signage or large industrial sheds.

Typical working heights can go beyond 100 ft on selected models, depending on availability.

Electric & Diesel Boom Lifts

Match power source to your environment

Electric boom lifts are quieter with low emissions, suitable for indoor or sensitive locations in Chennai. Diesel boom lifts suit outdoor, rough-terrain and long-duration construction work.

We’ll recommend boom lift models based on indoor/outdoor use, access width and floor conditions.

Truck-Mounted Boom Lifts

For road-side and multi-site work

Truck-mounted boom lifts are useful for signage, street lighting and jobs where you need to cover multiple locations in and around Chennai in a single day with faster mobilisation.

Common uses: highways, city streets, airport roads and maintenance along long stretches of façade.

Safety & inspection checks

Before a boom lift leaves for your Chennai site, key functions are checked – controls, hydraulics, emergency lowering and platform guardrails – in line with common industry guidelines for aerial work platforms.

Operators are requested to keep outriggers or stabilisers properly deployed, avoid overloading the platform and respect safe working distances from power lines.

Where you request an operator, we focus on trained personnel familiar with boom lift controls and basic fall-protection practices.
